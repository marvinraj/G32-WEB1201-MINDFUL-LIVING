About

Mindful Living

Mindful Living is a Malaysian based, independent lifestyle media brand providing over 100M readers with editorials on sustainability, slow living and self-care.

Our Misson

Established in Malaysia in 2022, our purpose is to cater to, support, and motivate our audience with impactful content on topics that are significant for leading a purposeful lifestyle.


Our website and daily newsletter, with a subscriber base of 100,000, have been featured in renowned publications like Forbes, Fast Company, and more, as a beloved lifestyle media brand that acknowledges the significance of self-care in conjunction with caring for the planet and community.
