The 5 Brands for
Sustainable Men’s Clothes

WRITTEN BY

MICHAEL

JULY 8, 2022

These ethical men’s clothing brands prioritize people and the planet, ensuring employees earn fair wages and that clothes are made with eco-friendly or sustainable materials. The styles are timeless, too, making these men’s collections well worth the investment

1. tentree

In search of men's clothing that makes a tangible impact? With tentree, every purchase contributes to the planting of 10 trees—a remarkable feat the Canadian company aims to achieve one billion times over by 2030. Embracing sustainability, they utilize a range of eco-friendly fabrics and blends, such as organic cotton and Tencel, in their clothing production. Explore their comfortable collection, knowing that your choices leave behind only a minimal footprint.

2. Pact

Originating from Boulder, Colorado, Pact is a sustainable apparel brand that excels in producing high-quality basics. They stand out by manufacturing all their products ethically in Fair Trade Certified factories, using 100 percent organic cotton. If you're seeking men's undergarments, Pact is your go-to destination. We assure you that their underwear and outerwear collection will become the epitome of softness and sustainability, elevating your wardrobe with essential pieces you'll cherish.

3.Levi's

For sustainable men's jeans, look no further than Levi's. Known for their iconic denim, Levi's prioritizes eco-friendly production by reducing water consumption, recycling vintage denim, and responsibly sourcing materials like cotton and Tencel™ Lyocell. Plus, they embrace inclusivity with a diverse range of sizes.

4. Everlane

If you seek high-quality staples such as tees, jeans, chinos, and button-downs, Everlane is your go-to destination. This one-stop shop offers a wide range of timeless basics that align with your ethical values, including organic cotton polos and dark-washed jeans free from harmful dyes and chemicals. Explore their collection of footwear and swimwear crafted from recycled materials, and while you're at it, consider upgrading your old backpack to a sustainable, well-crafted weekender. After all, you embody both style and substance.

5. Quince

Quince, a San Francisco-based brand, is our top pick for affordable and sustainable men's clothing. By shipping directly from their factories, they eliminate middlemen and reduce excessive packaging. Quince is committed to using eco-friendly materials, such as recycled polyester for activewear and organic cotton twill for chinos. With their stylish designs, affordability, and sustainability, Quince is the perfect choice for conscious shoppers.
